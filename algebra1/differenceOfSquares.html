Difference of Squares Formula

a² - b² = (a - b)(a + b)

Rearranging the remaining area into a rectangle.

Start with Square a (Area a²).
Cut out Square b (Area b²) from the corner.

Remaining Area: a² - b²

→

Take the green piece, flip it 90 degrees, and put it next to the blue piece. Now we have one large rectangle!
